About

AI Startup Cases

We are researchers obsessed with AI product/business mechanics. We translate lessons from leading AI startups into concise, verifiable case studies for founders, investors, and operators.

What we cover

  • • Market & users: demand scenarios, willingness to pay, channel mix.
  • • Product & tech: model choices, data sources, product form factors.
  • • Revenue & costs: pricing, margin, COSS/SaaS/subscription mixes.
  • • Growth & compliance: activation, retention, policy/IP risks.

Update cadence

We add 2-3 deep cases weekly. Major product/policy updates are reflected within 48 hours.

Independence

How to use this site

Analyses are for research/education only; not investment advice. Validate with your own user research, compliance checks, and unit economics before acting.

For reuse in commercial contexts, credit the source. For reprints, email hi@ai-startup-cases.com.
